## Daily Shop Open & Close Checklist

### Follow the Pi Method
This checklist is intended to establish consistent daily open and close routines following the Pi Method for Get That Art LLC.

### Morning Checklist
- [ ] Unlock and disable the security system.
- [ ] Power on all lights and equipment.
- [ ] Check appointment book and prepare workstation for the day.
- [ ] Restock supplies as needed.
- [ ] Ensure hygiene protocols are in place.

### Evening Checklist
- [ ] Safely shut down and clean all workstations.
- [ ] Restock supplies for the next day.
- [ ] Turn off all lights and non-essential equipment.
- [ ] Lock and enable the security system.
- [ ] Perform end-of-day reporting and accounting.
