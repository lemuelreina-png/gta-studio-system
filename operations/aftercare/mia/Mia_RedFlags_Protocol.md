# MIA RED FLAG PROTOCOL
Get That Art Studios

---

## PURPOSE

Identify and respond to healing risks immediately.

Protect:
- Client health
- Tattoo quality
- Studio liability

---

## 🚨 IMMEDIATE REMOVAL TRIGGERS

Client MUST remove Derm Shield if:

- Leakage from bandage
- Spreading redness
- Increasing pain or heat
- Rash / itching / blisters
- Foul smell
- Yellow or green discharge

---

## ⚠️ ACTION STEPS (CLIENT)

1. Remove bandage immediately
2. Wash gently with soap + water
3. Pat dry
4. Contact studio if unsure

---

## 🔴 ESCALATION (MEDICAL)

Client must seek medical attention if:

- Symptoms worsen rapidly
- Fever develops
- Severe swelling or pain
- Infection suspected

---

## 🧠 STAFF RESPONSE

When client reports issue:

1. Identify symptom
2. Confirm removal of bandage
3. Guide proper cleaning
4. Document issue
5. Escalate if needed

---

## 📋 DOCUMENTATION

Log:
- Date
- Symptoms
- Action taken
- Outcome

---

## 🔒 LIABILITY CONTROL

Clear instruction = reduced risk  
Ignored protocol = increased risk  

We document EVERYTHING

---

## PI LAW

“Early action prevents damage.”

Delay = complication

---

## FINAL RULE

If it looks wrong  
→ It is wrong  
→ Remove + reassess immediately
