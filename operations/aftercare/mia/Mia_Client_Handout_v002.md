Get That Art Studios

Your tattoo is a fresh wound. Proper aftercare = proper healing.

---

## 🛡️ DERM SHIELD METHOD (YOUR BANDAGE)

### DAY 0 (Tattoo Day)
- Derm Shield applied by artist
- Leave it on for ~24 hours
- Fluid buildup is NORMAL

---

### DAY 1
- Remove in the shower
- Wash gently (light soap)
- Rinse fully
- PAT DRY completely

➡️ Apply new Derm Shield

---

### DAY 2–5
- Keep bandage on IF sealed
- Showering is OK
- DO NOT remove unless needed

---

### DAY 5–7
- Remove bandage
- Begin normal healing care

---

## ⚠️ REMOVE IMMEDIATELY IF:

- Leakage from bandage
- Red rash / itching / blisters
- Increasing pain or heat
- Bad smell
- Yellow or green fluid

➡️ Action:
- Remove
- Wash
- Contact us if needed

---

## 🚫 DO NOT:

- Touch with dirty hands
- Pick or scratch
- Soak (no baths, pools, ocean)
- Wear tight clothing over it
- Overuse products

---

## 🧴 AFTER BANDAGE REMOVAL

- Wash 1–2x daily
- Use LIGHT, fragrance-free moisturizer
- Let it breathe

---

## 🔥 HEALING PHASE

- Peeling and itching = normal
- DO NOT pick
- Keep clean + moisturized
