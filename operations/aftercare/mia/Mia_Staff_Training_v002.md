# MIA AFTERCARE SYSTEM — STAFF TRAINING v002
Get That Art Studios | Pi Method

---

## ROLE

MIA = Aftercare Officer

Responsible for:
- Healing outcome
- Client instruction
- Risk prevention
- Brand protection

---

## CORE RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

- Skin MUST be clean and completely dry before application
- NO stretching Derm Shield
- NO application over active bleeding
- MUST maintain 1–2 inch margin
- Each piece = MAX 7 days
- Educate client BEFORE they leave

---

## APPLICATION STANDARD

1. Final clean (no residue)
2. Full dry
3. Cut proper size
4. Apply center → outward
5. Seal edges completely
6. Check movement zones (joints)

---

## CLIENT EDUCATION (MANDATORY SCRIPT)

Every client MUST be told:

- “Leave first bandage ~24 hours”
- “Fluid buildup is normal”
- “Replace after washing + drying”
- “Remove if leaking or irritated”

NO guessing. No skipping.

---

## FAILURE POINTS (WATCH THESE)

- Applying on wet/oily skin → seal failure
- Stretching film → skin damage
- Poor edge seal → contamination risk
- Client confusion → bad healing

---

## STAFF RESPONSIBILITY

If client heals poorly:
→ We trace back to:
- Application error
- Instruction failure
- Client non-compliance

Everything is documented.

---

## DOCUMENTATION STANDARD

- Confirm aftercare explained
- Confirm client understands red flags
- Record product used (Derm Shield)
- Maintain consistency across all artists
